Privacy Policy


The Tourism Management System is committed to safeguarding the privacy and personal information of all its users. When users register, book services, or interact with the system, certain personal details such as names, addresses, phone numbers, email IDs, and payment information may be collected. This data is gathered only to facilitate travel planning, reservations, communication, and to provide a seamless tourism experience. We ensure that all personal information is stored securely and is protected from unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. Data will only be shared with trusted service providers—such as hotels, airlines, transport agencies, and tour operators—strictly for the purpose of completing bookings or delivering requested services. The system does not sell, trade, or distribute user data to third parties for marketing or commercial purposes without explicit consent. Any information disclosed is in compliance with applicable laws and regulations. Users also have the right to review, update, or request deletion of their personal information at any time. Additionally, the Tourism Management System uses encryption and other security practices to safeguard online transactions and sensitive data. By using the system, users agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this privacy policy, which may be updated periodically to reflect new security practices or legal requirements
